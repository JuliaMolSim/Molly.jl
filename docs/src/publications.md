# Publications

## Citation

If you use Molly, please cite the following paper ([bib entry here](https://github.com/JuliaMolSim/Molly.jl/blob/master/CITATION.bib)):

- Greener JG. Differentiable simulation to develop molecular dynamics force fields for disordered proteins, [Chemical Science](https://doi.org/10.1039/D3SC05230C) 15, 4897-4909 (2024)

A paper involving more contributors with further details on the software will be written at some point.

## General resources

Some general resources for learning molecular dynamics include:
- Molecular Modelling: Principles And Applications textbook by Andrew Leach (2001)
- [Introduction to Statistical Mechanics](https://peastman.github.io/statmech/index.html) by Peter Eastman (2014)
- [Best Practices for Foundations in Molecular Simulations](https://doi.org/10.33011/livecoms.1.1.5957) article (2019)
- [Best Practices for Alchemical Free Energy Calculations](https://doi.org/10.33011/livecoms.2.1.18378) article (2020)
- [Gromacs documentation](https://www.gromacs.org)
- [OpenMM documentation](https://openmm.org)

Papers relevant to particular functions and structs are referenced in the docstrings. See the documentation home page for some talk recordings and slides about Molly.
